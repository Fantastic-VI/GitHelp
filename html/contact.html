Higher Council for the Rights of Persons with Disabilities (HCD)

Higher Council for the Rights of Persons with Disabilities The Supreme Council for the Rights of Persons with Disabilities was established under the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Law No. 31 of 2007 under the name (Higher Council for the Affairs of Persons with Disabilities) as a public institution headed by His Highness Prince Raad bin Zaid, and under the presidency of His Highness Prince Mired bin Raad bin Zaid, the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Law was issued No. 20 of 2017, according to which the name of the Council was amended to become Higher Council for Rights.
