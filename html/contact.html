Friendship Association for the Blind

Foundation Year: 1977 Goals:-
  1. Improving the financial and psychological situation of the blind person and investing the resources of the financial association for his benefit
  2. Providing job opportunities for the blind in both the public and private sectors, commensurate with their abilities and capabilities, and reducing the obstacles that stand in their way.
  3. Establishing centers for the rehabilitation of the blind and specially designed libraries for their use, giving them the opportunity to practice their hobbies and acquire multiple types of experiences.
  4. Spreading social awareness in the community to improve the behavior and perception of the society towards the status of the blind.
  5. Providing housing for the blind and helping them start their own projects.Their own projects.
  6. Establishing kindergartens for children with visual impairment, in addition to basic schools for students of both genders.
  7. Granting loans to the blind, whether employed or unemployed, for the purposes of job rehabilitation, education, medical treatment and emergency cases.
